|ཧྥེན་ཡང་ཞེས་པའི་ཡུལ་གྲུ་ཞིག་ན་རུས་ཕེ་ཚང་ལ་མནའ་མ་ཞིག་ཡོད་པ་དེས་ཚེ་གང་ལ་བློ་རྩེ་འོད་དཔག་མེད་ལ་གཏད་དེ་འོད་མཚན་བཏོན།<br>གྲངས་ཡང་སྲན་མ་ཆུང་ངུ་ཡིས་བགྲང་པར་བྱས་ཏེ་སྲན་ཆུང་བསགས་པ་ན་བྲེ་བཅུ་གསུམ་གང་བར་གྱུར། | In a region called Hfen Yang, there was a woman who belonged to the Fe clan. Throughout her entire life, she focused her mind on the buddha Amitābha and recited his name. To count her recitations, she used small lentils. When put all together, they amounted to thirteen dre measures. | In Fazlullah’s country there was a woman in the family who gave her the name of Amitabha to her life.<br>The number is counted as small beans and when small beans accumulate, it becomes thirteen bushels.|

It’s time for me to go to Sukhāvatī.” She then straightened her body and passed away.<br>It is said that just that moment, flowers of five colors rained from the sky, and all the local people witnessed auspicious signs. <br>This was translated from Chinese into Tibetan by Khenmo Dawa Drolma. | When she died, she said to the householders, “Since the buddhas have given me lotus seats, I will go to Sukhavati.”<br>Immediately after that, a rain of five kinds of flowers fell from the sky and everyone witnessed auspicious signs as auspicious signs.<br>The abbot Dawa Dolma translated the Tibetan script from Chinese script.|

|ཁྱེད་ལ་བསྟོད་ཅིང་གསོལ་བ་བཏབ་པའི་མཐུས༔<br>བདག་གི་ཚེ་བསོད་དཔལ་འབྱོར་རྒྱས་པ་དང་༔<br>འཇིགས་པ་བཅུ་དྲུག་རྐྱེན་ངན་བར་ཆད་སོལ༔|Through the power of praising and praying to you, May my lifespan, merit, splendour and wealth increase, And the sixteen fears, adversity and obstacles be dispelled. | Through the power of praising and praying to you<br>Increase my lifespan merit and prosperity<br>Remove the sixteen fears and obstacles of adversity.|
|འགྲོ་ལ་གནོད་པའི་གདོན་འགེགས་འབྱུང་པོ་ཐུལ༔<br>བསྟན་ལ་འཚེ་བྱེད་དམ་སྲི་མ་ལུས་པ༔<br>མ་ཁྱོད་ཐུགས་རྗེ་ནུས་མཐུའི་སྟོབས་རྩལ་གྱིས༔<br>མ་ལུས་ཞི་ཞིང་བསྟན་པ་རྒྱས་པར་མཛོད༔ | Subjugate spirits and disruptive forces who prey upon beings,<br>And may all those damsi demons who would harm the teachings<br>Be pacified through the force of your compassionate power and strength,<br>So that the teachings spread far and wide. | Subjugate harmful spirits and elemental spirits who harm beings.<br>May all those who cause harm to the teachings<br>Mother, through the power and strength of your compassion<br>Pacify and spread the teachings! |
|བསྟན་འཛིན་དམ་པའི་སྐུ་ཚེ་བསྲིང་བ་དང་༔<br>དགེ་འདུན་སྡེ་ཚོགས་འཕེལ་བར་བྱིན་གྱིས་རློབས༔ | Grant your blessings, so that the lives of the noble holders of the teachings may be extended,<br>And the saṅgha community may flourish and grow. | May the lives of the holy holders of the teachings be prolonged,<br>Grant your blessings so that the saṅgha saṅgha may increase! |
